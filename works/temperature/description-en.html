東京の夏は徐々に暑く・長くなっている

東京における過去142年の日別平均気温

Summer in Tokyo is Gradually Becoming Hotter and Longer

Daily average temperature in Tokyo over the past 140 years

気象庁の公表している6月〜9月における日別平均気温を過去142年分ヒートマップで表現した。

この作品は東洋経済オンラインにて公開されています。

This heat map indicates the average daily temperatures of the summer season (June to September) in Tokyo over the course of 140 years. You can hover over or tap on a cell to see detailed information. Each cell indicates the average temperature on the day by 2°, from blue (less than 18°C) to deep red (30°C or more). The raw data were obtained from the Japan Meteorological Agency. This visualization shows that the temperature in Tokyo has been steadily rising and now approaching a deadly level.

This work is published in Toyo Keizai Online [Japanese].
