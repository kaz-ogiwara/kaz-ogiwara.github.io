Elections and Dissolutions in The G7

Timeline of elections and dissolutions in the G7 countries after WWII

This is a timeline of elections and dissolutions in the G7 countries after WWII.

While Prime Minister of Japan virtually has a right to dissolve The House of Representatives, it has been often criticized to be arbitrary. This visualisation shows national elections of G7 countries after WWII, focusing on causes of elections.

Data source: Takazawa, 2016

Languages and libraries: JavaScript (jQuery), SQL (MySQL) and PHP for retrieving and shaping data
