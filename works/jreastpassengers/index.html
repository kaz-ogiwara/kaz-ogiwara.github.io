JR East Average Daily Passengers Map

September 2018

Show full screen

This is the map of average daily passengers at 950 stations in the business area of JR East, one of Japanese railway companies. The raw data is published by the company, then latitudes and longitudes were obtained with Google Geocoding API. The map was drawn with Leaflet.js, and map tiles are provided by OpenStreetMap.

While the target area is only JR East's business area from Kanto to Tohoku, it clearly shows the population concentration in Tokyo metropolitan area. The raw data page lists stations in the order of decreasing average daily passengers, and 99 stations of 100 are in Tokyo area (Tokyo + peripheral 3 prefectures). The only exception is Sendai, 49th, prefectoral capital of Miyagi.
