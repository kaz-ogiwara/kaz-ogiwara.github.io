3D map of Tokyo Land Prices

The latest price and comparison with the last year

3D map of the official land prices in Tokyo as of July 1, 2018.

You can change the latest price or comparison with the last year, and select the purpose of the land (no preference, for residence, or for business). By hovering (or tapping) each bar, detailed information about the area is shown; closest station and distance, price or percent change, size, and purpose.

This is a public work. The app and the explanation are at Toyo Keizai Online [Japanese].

Data source: The Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, Transport, and Tourism

Languages and libraries: deck.gl, JavaScript (P5.js, jQuery), SQL (MySQL) and PHP for retrieving and handling data
