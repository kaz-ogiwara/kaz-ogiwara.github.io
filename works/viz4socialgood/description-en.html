Viz for Social Good: Visualization of Human Trafficking

Interactive sankey diagram, map and details for Viz for Social Good

Data source: The Counter-Trafficking Data Collaborative (CTDC)

Languages and libraries: JavaScript (P5.js, jQuery), SQL (MySQL) and PHP for retrieving and shaping data
