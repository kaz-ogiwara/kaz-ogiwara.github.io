Viz for Social Good: Visualization of Human Trafficking

"But you do not know where I came from or where I am going"

- Interactive sankey diagram, map and details for Viz for Social Good

- By hovering (or tapping) side bar, detailed information about the people from / to the country is shown.

- This visualization is optimized for PC (with 960+px width), sorry!

- Data source: The Counter-Trafficking Data Collaborative (CTDC)

- Numbers are total of all years of registration (2002 - 2017).

- Virtually invalid values such as "-99" or "Unknown" are removed.

- Thus, the total number is not equal to sum of each items.
