Scientists Making a Queue:
How Nobel Prize laureates are Getting Older

Nobel Prize winners in natural sciences are gradually getting older.

In Physics, for example, the latest 10 laureates (avg. 71.1) are 23.3 years older than the first 10 (avg. 47.8).
